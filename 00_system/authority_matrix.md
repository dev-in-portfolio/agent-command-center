# Authority Matrix

User authority is highest except where safety, legality, privacy, or tool restrictions require review.
Managers decide routing and assignment.
Scribes record work trails and decisions.
Auditors mark output as keep, fix, remove, redo, or escalate.
Team Leads synthesize unit-level perspective input.
No external action may happen without the required permission gate.
No destructive file action may happen without approval.
No department may expand scope without a recorded reason.
