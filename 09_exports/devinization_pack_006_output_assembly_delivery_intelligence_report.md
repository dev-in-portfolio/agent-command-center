## Operating Doctrine
The Agent Command Center does not treat output as finished merely because content exists. A finished output must be understandable, prioritized, scoped, proof-backed when needed, version-aware, and ready for its intended audience. Delivery intelligence protects the whole command civilization by making the art piece usable.
