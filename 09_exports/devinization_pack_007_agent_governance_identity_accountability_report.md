## Operating Doctrine
The Agent Command Center does not treat agents as trustworthy just because they have names, roles, or confident output. Trust must be earned through scoped execution, proof, validator performance, repair behavior, and repeated alignment with Devin’s operating rules. Governance keeps the agent civilization coherent without shrinking the art piece.
